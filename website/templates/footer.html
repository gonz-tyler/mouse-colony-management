How the System Works

Welcome to Mouse Manager! Here's how the system works:

  • Register for an account to get started.
  • Log in to access your dashboard.
  • Manage your mouse colony by adding, editing, or removing records.
  • Track breeding pairs and offspring details.
  • Generate reports for your research or administrative needs.
  • Use the search and filter functionality to quickly find specific records.
  • Request breeding or culling actions through the Requests section.
  • Receive notifications for any updates or actions required.
  • Create and manage user accounts with different roles.
  • Create teams and manage mice together.
